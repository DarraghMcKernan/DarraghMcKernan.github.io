Introduction:

Teams is a website created by Microsoft and is part of the Microsoft Suite of sites such as outlook and word.

Features like live video calling and teams allow users to have conversations with groups of people all at the same time,

this allows for instantaneous feedback and is incredibly useful when sharing ideas or work.
