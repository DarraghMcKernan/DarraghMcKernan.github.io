Usability Assesment

Basic test - Upload an assignment to teams.

For the first test I will ask my mam to upload an assignment to teams using an image from my laptops files.

My mam is not very familiar with computers.

I started my mam off on the front page of Teams on the 'teams' menu and asked her to upload an assignment.
She immediately navigated to the assignments icon and pressed it. From here she pressed on the top assignment
and pressed on attach which brings up the file explorer to select an file to upload. This concluded the test.

For the second test I asked my older brother to upload an assignment too.

My brother is very familiar with computer but is not familiar with teams.

I also started my brother off at the 'teams' menu and asked him to upload an assignment. He too pressed on
assignments very quickly and then clicked on the first assignment and selected attatch.This concluded the test.

Advanced test - Change profile picture.

For this test I asked my mam to change profile picture and started her on the calender screen where she left off.
Sheimmediately navigated to the profile icon in the top right corner afte a bit of looking around and pressed the
icon again to change profile picture. This concluded the test
I again asked my brother to do the same as my mam and he very quickly went to the profile picture and found the
prompt to change profile picture.

Expert test - Create a meeting.

For the this test I asked my mam to create a meeting using teams.
I again started my mam on the 'teams' menu and asked her to make a meeting.
After a while of pressing on various menus my mam ended up on calender and seen the New Meeting button and
pressed it. She filled in the details and I ended the test here.
I now asked my brother to do the same thing and he too spent a while searching around on the site before finding
the New Meeting button on the calender. He filled in the details and I ended the test.

Summary.

Overall i found teams to be a very well designed website that was easy to use and accessible even to people that

wouldn't be as familiar with computers. I did not discover any problems with the site as it was so well labelled

that it was easy to navigate. I feel that after using the site for even 10 minutes most users will be capable of

Performing all tests with ease. I would have liked to have done a Create a new team test but because I am a

student I did not have the ability to do so. I feel this would have been an appropriate expert test.
