Conclusion

In conclusion I feel that teams is a very well designed website. It makes sense that it is so popular as it is so easy to

use and pleasant to look at. Uploading assignments is quick and its clear every step of the way what is happening and what

needs to happen next. The uploading bar is a massive help especially for someone with slow internet like myself its nice to

be able to see that progress is being made. The calendar menu is very useful especially when there are lots of online classes

scheduled for a day and also for showing when homework was due. The layout of teams is simplistic but detailed enough to show

all the information that the average user is going to need all in one place. It is clear that they prioritised these features

that are commonly used as features like the apps page are clearly underdeveloped due to the fact that the userbase for it is

probably a very small percentage compared to the userbase using the other functions of the site.
